Root finding

Root Finding Without Derivatives: Bisection, Bracket and TOMS748
Root Finding With Derivatives: Newton-Raphson, Halley & Schroeder
Examples of Root-Finding (with and without derivatives)
Locating Function Minima using Brent's algorithm

Several tools are provided to aid finding minima and roots of functions.

Some root-finding without derivatives methods are Bisection, bracket and solve including use of TOMS 748 algorithm.

For root-finding with derivatives the methods of Newton-Raphson iteration Halley and Schroeder are implemented.

For locating minima of a function, Brent's method is provided.

Also provided are root-finding examples:

  • cube root-finding without derivatives
  • root-finding with 1st derivatives
  • root-finding with 1st and 2nd derivatives
  • Brent's method for a simple function.
